A list of science fiction film s released in the 1960s. These films include core elements of science fiction , but can cross into other genres. They have been released to a cinema audience by the commercial film industry and are widely distributed with reviews by reputable critics. Collectively, the science fiction films from the 1960s received five Academy Awards , a Hugo Award and a BAFTA Award .
